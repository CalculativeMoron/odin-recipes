How to Make Nuclear Waste

  • Uranium

    First we'll need some nice uranium.
    Make sure to get it from an anonymous
    channel so the government doesn't
    suspect a thing.

  • Location

    To make sure no one else knows what
    we're doing, we have to find a place for all
    of this. Make sure it's isolated from the rest
    of the world.
    (Note: You'll need a facility.)

  • Bomb

    Getting the uranium and finding a good
    location are only a few of the hurdles you'll
    face, because next you have to make a bomb-
    an atomic bomb. Good luck!
    (Note: Also prepare a bomber, you'll need it!)

  • Drop

    After somehow making the atomic bomb,
    you'll have to find another location to drop it.
    Preferably you'll want to drop it close enough
    to a large, populated country to cause public
    panic, but far away enough so said country can't
    defend against it.

  • Detonate

    After finding a place to drop the bomb, just set
    the bomber in place and send the signal, then hope you
    don't get caught. Make sure to immedately flee
    the place where the facility the bomb was made after
    sendng off the bomber.
    (Note: You should have a plane/jet or
    underground railway system on the side.)

  • Assassinate

    Assuming no one beside your allies knows you
    planned the attack, try and fool the goverment to
    allow you to join in the inspection team. If that
    doesn't work then you're free to assassinate one
    of the people who were gonna be in the team and
    disguise as them.

  • Waste

    Still assuming, if you succesful make it into the team, than before they set all of you off to the site, set up some kind of storage area in your hazmat suit where you'll hide the nuclear waste. When you reach the site, collect the waste like the other's, but secretly hide them in the suit. After all that, go somewhere safe, store the waste, and let the government handle everything else. Now you have your nuclear waste, what are you gonna do with it? I don't know, but it looks like a cake.
